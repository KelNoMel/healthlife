Tube Filling

We can offer a contract filling and packing service for a wide range of cream, gel and liquid products using jars, tubes and bottles. Our contract packing services cater for any size or style of product.

Our automated emulsification system allows us to produce creams of the highest quality in house. We offer drum sizes from 5L to 2000L per batch and are able to provide quantities to our customers specifications.

Additionally, we also provide packing services for cosmetic products. We have a total of 12 packing lines with 4 high speed packing machines, capable of producing 12,000 jars per day per packing line. The packing volumes range from 15mL to 500mL.

We also offer labelling services. Our fully automatic packaging and labelling lines are capable of producing 12,000 units per day with sizes range from 25ml to 2000ml. Batch sizes range from 2000 units.
