Welcome to Healthlife

Health Life Pharmaceuticals is one of the major contract manufacturers of quality healthcare supplements and skin care pharmaceutical products. It features a 10,000 square metre state-of-the-art manufacturing facility. Health Life Pharmaceuticals is a Therapeutic Goods Administration (TGA) certified and Good Manufacturing Practice (GMP) licensed manufacturer with the capacity to formulate and package premium quality and authentically Australian products. We provide a variety of contract manufacturing services for pharmaceutical, cosmetics and supplements manufacturing, including product development, filling, packaging, regulation consulting and product design. We will work closely with you to bring your ideas to life.
