Health Life Pharmaceuticals Australia Pty Ltd is a privately owned Australian company, based in the western suburbs of Sydney. Health Life Pharmaceuticals is one of the major contract manufacturers of quality healthcare supplements and skincare pharmaceutical products and features a 10,000 square metre state-of-the-art manufacturing facility. Health Life Pharmaceuticals is a Therapeutic Goods Administration (TGA) certified and Good Manufacturing Practice (GMP) licensed manufacturer with the capacity to formulate and package premium quality and authentically Australian products. We also hold a National Industrial Chemicals Notification and Assessment Scheme (NICNAS) Licence, designed to help protect workers, the public and the environment from the harmful effects of industrial chemicals.

Health Life Pharmaceuticals’ output broadly comprises healthcare complementary products and cosmetic products which include tablets, hard gelatin capsules, soft gelatin capsules, liquids, creams, ointments, facial masks, and more. Additionally, Health Life Pharmaceuticals also supplies bar soaps, milk powder products and infant formula products.

Our team has over 20 years of professional experience in the pharmaceutical and cosmetic manufacturing industry. Health Life Pharmaceuticals has contracted for many Australian and international brands and has collaborated with leading laboratories and research institutes. Our goal is to supply our customers with the products of the highest quality.
