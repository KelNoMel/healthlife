    • Can I bring my own formulation and packaging?

    Yes! You can absolutely bring your own formulation and packaging. We provide a range of services from formulation to filling to packing so our clients can select the services that best fit their needs.

    Alternatively, if you don’t have your own formulation or packaging, we can work with you to develop a unique formula and design packaging for your product.

    Can you help with formulation and packaging?

    Absolutely! Our team can work closely with you to develop unique formulas and packaging for your products. Our product development team has over 20 years of experience formulating cosmetics and skincare and can help you bring your product to life. Our in-house design team can assist with product design, branding, logo design, product packaging, creating marketing materials and more. We understand that beautiful packaging and product design can make your product stand out. Our team will work closely with you to ensure that you have all the resources to bring your product to market.

    How long does it take to make a formula?

    The cost for each new project is unique and cant be estimated with any accuracy without all information. Our product brief form should be completed so we understand all the variables from ingredients, product profile, packaging types, production volumes and any special requirements you may have.

    On average we complete formulations within 8-14 weeks depending on the complexity of the project. Once we have reviewed your brief we can provide more specific guidance.

    How much does it cost to manufacture my products?

    The cost varies depending on the selected ingredients, packaging, production volumes and other requirements. Please contact us with your product and service specifications so we can provide you a quote.

    Can you make samples?

    Yes! We can make samples of your products for you. We are also capable of doing small runs of product as our smallest drum size is 5kg. Please contact us with your product requirements so we can provide you with a tailored solution.

    What quality testing do you use?

    Quality is of the highest importance at Health Life Pharmaceuticals. We do a variety of testing in-house including, but not limited to, shelf-life studies, microbiological testing, physical and analytical testing, and heavy metals and toxicity testing. We also have the resources to organise external testing if required.

    What are the manufacturing lead times?

    Production is typically completed within 6-8 weeks from receipt of all packaging and raw materials, though this can vary depending on production volume.

    How do I get a quote for my product?

    Contact us through our contact form with your product requirements and we will get back to you as soon as possible with a tailored quote and manufacturing solution.
